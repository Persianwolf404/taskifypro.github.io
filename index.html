    Your Privacy, Our Commitment

    At Taskify, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure online experience. We collect necessary data, such as personal information and usage data, to understand your needs and provide you with the best service possible. Rest assured, your data will be used solely for project-related communication, analytics, and personalization, and we take stringent measures to safeguard it through encryption and security practices. We do not access your website after project completion. To ensure project security, we require a 20% prepayment, which is retained in the event of project cancellation, with the remaining 80% due upon project completion. We offer two complimentary code edits, and for additional edits, we provide support at a reasonable cost. Our 24/7 support is available to assist you at any time. Please note that clients are responsible for hosting and domain costs. Additionally, we have a disclaimer regarding website content, which clarifies that the content on your website is your sole responsibility. We take your privacy and data security seriously and are committed to providing you with the highest quality service.
